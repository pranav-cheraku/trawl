This Privacy Policy explains how Pranav Cheraku ("Trawl", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use Trawl, our product-feedback analysis and spec-generation service (the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## Information We Collect

**Account information.** You sign in to Trawl using Google. When you do, we receive your name, email address, profile-picture URL, and Google account identifier.

**Content you provide.** We store the content you create and upload, including project names and descriptions, the feedback sources you connect, feedback and reviews ingested into your projects, generated specifications, and chat conversations.

**Billing information.** When you purchase credits, we store your credit balance and transaction records. Payment-card details are collected and processed solely by Stripe; Trawl never receives or stores your full card number.

**Technical information.** We use a strictly necessary session cookie to keep you signed in, and your browser's local storage to remember interface preferences. We do not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking cookies.

**Third-party content.** Trawl ingests publicly available reviews and feedback — for example, from app stores or public forums — into your projects. This content may include information authored by third parties, such as reviewer usernames.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide, operate, and maintain the Service;
- Authenticate your account and keep you signed in;
- Generate specifications and answers using artificial intelligence;
- Process payments and manage your credit balance;
- Communicate with you about your account or the Service;
- Improve and secure the Service.

## Cookies and Local Storage

Trawl uses a single strictly necessary cookie to maintain your authenticated session. We also use your browser's local storage to save interface preferences. We do not use cookies for analytics or advertising.
